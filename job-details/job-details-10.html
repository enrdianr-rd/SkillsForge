Security Guard Job Description

Description

We are seeking a vigilant and responsible Security Guard to join our team. The Security Guard will be responsible for maintaining a safe and secure environment for our staff, customers, and visitors. The ideal candidate will have strong observational skills, good judgment, and the ability to respond to emergencies calmly and efficiently.

Responsibilities

  • Patrol premises regularly to maintain order and detect signs of intrusion or damage
  • Monitor surveillance cameras and alarms
  • Control access points and ensure only authorized individuals enter the premises
  • Respond to alarms and security incidents in a timely manner
  • Escort employees, customers, or visitors as needed
  • Report any suspicious activities or incidents to management

Qualifications

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Proven experience as a security guard or relevant position
  • Valid security guard license
  • Strong observational and surveillance skills
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Ability to handle stressful situations calmly and effectively
