Nurse Job Description

Description

We are seeking a compassionate and skilled nurse to join our healthcare team. The ideal candidate will be dedicated to providing high-quality patient care and will possess strong clinical skills. The nurse will be responsible for assessing patient needs, administering medication, and collaborating with healthcare professionals to develop and implement care plans.

Responsibilities

  • Assess patient health problems and needs
  • Develop and implement nursing care plans
  • Administer medications and treatments
  • Monitor and report patient symptoms and changes in condition
  • Educate patients and their families about health conditions and treatments
  • Collaborate with healthcare professionals to provide comprehensive care

Qualifications

  • Registered Nurse (RN) license
  • Bachelor's degree in Nursing preferred
  • Previous nursing experience preferred
  • Strong clinical skills
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Ability to work well in a team environment
