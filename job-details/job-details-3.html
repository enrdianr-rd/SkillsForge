Civil Engineer Job Description

Description

We are seeking a highly skilled and experienced Civil Engineer to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for designing, planning, and overseeing construction projects, such as roads, bridges, and buildings. The Civil Engineer will collaborate with architects, contractors, and other professionals to ensure projects are completed on time and within budget.

Responsibilities

  • Develop project plans and specifications
  • Perform calculations and analysis for structural design
  • Ensure compliance with building codes and regulations
  • Oversee construction activities and monitor progress
  • Review and approve project designs and changes
  • Prepare and present progress reports to stakeholders

Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering or related field
  • Licensed Professional Engineer (PE) preferred
  • Minimum of 5 years of relevant experience
  • Strong knowledge of engineering principles and practices
  • Proficiency in CAD software and other design tools
  • Excellent communication and project management skills
