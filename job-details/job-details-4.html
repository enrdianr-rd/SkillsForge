Electrician Job Description

Description

We are looking for a skilled Electrician to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for installing, maintaining, and repairing electrical wiring, equipment, and fixtures. The Electrician will work in a variety of settings, including residential, commercial, and industrial environments.

Responsibilities

  • Install, maintain, and repair electrical systems and components
  • Inspect electrical systems and equipment for safety and compliance
  • Diagnose and troubleshoot electrical issues
  • Read and interpret electrical blueprints and schematics
  • Perform routine maintenance on electrical systems
  • Adhere to safety and quality standards

Qualifications

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Completion of an electrician apprenticeship program preferred
  • State-issued electrician license/certification
  • Minimum of 2 years of experience as an electrician
  • Strong knowledge of electrical systems and components
  • Ability to work independently and in a team environment
