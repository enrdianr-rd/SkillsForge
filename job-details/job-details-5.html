Plumber Job Description

Description

We are seeking a skilled Plumber to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for installing, repairing, and maintaining plumbing systems in residential and commercial buildings. The Plumber will work on a variety of projects, from fixing leaks and unclogging drains to installing new pipes and fixtures.

Responsibilities

  • Install, repair, and maintain plumbing systems and fixtures
  • Determine the cause of plumbing issues and propose solutions
  • Read blueprints and specifications to determine layout of plumbing systems
  • Perform inspections and preventative maintenance on plumbing systems
  • Collaborate with other construction professionals, such as electricians and carpenters, as needed
  • Adhere to safety and building codes and regulations

Qualifications

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Completion of a plumbing apprenticeship program preferred
  • State-issued plumbing license/certification
  • Minimum of 2 years of experience as a plumber
  • Strong knowledge of plumbing systems and components
  • Ability to work independently and in a team environment
