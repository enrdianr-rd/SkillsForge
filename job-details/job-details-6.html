Grocery Clerk Job Description

Description

We are looking for a reliable and customer-oriented Grocery Clerk to join our team. The Grocery Clerk will assist customers with locating products, stocking shelves, and maintaining a clean and organized store environment. The ideal candidate will have excellent communication skills and a strong attention to detail.

Responsibilities

  • Assist customers in locating products and answering inquiries
  • Stock and replenish shelves, displays, and coolers
  • Ensure product freshness and quality by rotating stock
  • Maintain cleanliness and organization of aisles, shelves, and storage areas
  • Operate cash registers and handle transactions accurately
  • Assist with receiving and unloading merchandise shipments

Qualifications

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Previous experience in a retail or grocery environment preferred
  • Excellent customer service skills
  • Ability to lift and carry heavy objects
  • Basic math and cash handling skills
  • Flexibility to work evenings, weekends, and holidays
