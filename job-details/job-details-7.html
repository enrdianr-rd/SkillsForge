Delivery Driver Job Description

Description

We are seeking a reliable and customer-focused Delivery Driver to join our team. The Delivery Driver will be responsible for transporting goods and packages to customers in a timely and safe manner. The ideal candidate will have a valid driver's license, a clean driving record, and excellent communication skills.

Responsibilities

  • Deliver a wide variety of items to different addresses
  • Load, unload, prepare, inspect, and operate delivery vehicle
  • Follow delivery routes and time schedules
  • Interact with customers in a professional and courteous manner
  • Collect payments and handle returns or exchanges, if needed
  • Maintain delivery vehicle and report any issues or incidents

Qualifications

  • Valid driver's license
  • Clean driving record
  • Previous experience as a delivery driver or relevant position preferred
  • Ability to lift and carry heavy items
  • Excellent time management and organizational skills
  • Customer service-oriented mindset
