Cashier Job Description

Description

We are seeking a friendly and efficient Cashier to join our team. The Cashier will handle cash transactions, process payments using various methods, and provide excellent customer service. The ideal candidate will have strong math skills, attention to detail, and the ability to work in a fast-paced environment.

Responsibilities

  • Process sales transactions accurately and efficiently
  • Accept payments in cash, credit, or check
  • Issue receipts, refunds, or change to customers
  • Maintain a clean and organized checkout area
  • Resolve customer complaints or escalate issues as needed
  • Assist customers with locating products or answering inquiries

Qualifications

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Previous experience as a cashier or in a similar role preferred
  • Strong math and numerical skills
  • Excellent customer service and communication skills
  • Ability to multitask and work in a fast-paced environment
  • Attention to detail and accuracy
